Beatrice: Home

Beatrice is a personal project that arose from a need as a new Home Owner. How does one keep track of all the appliances in the home?

There are Serial Numbers, Warranty Information, Filters to change etc etc!

Storing this information in Excel seemed like a good place to start, but it was boring!


Enter: Beatrice: Home



What is Beatrice?

Beatrice will help you catalog details of the appliances in your home along with the room they are in (optional)
You start out by creating a profile for your home; then add the appliance details like brand, type and date of purchase
You can then choose to add pictures of the Serial Number, Warranty Details and Purchase Receipt
Beatrice will even help keep track of reminders for certain appliances; like replacing the water filter in the Refrigerator
Best of all there are no Ads and it is free! :)

Technology

Beatrice is built using the Ionic Framework, AngularJS and Firebase by Google.
It is avaliable for both Android and iOS!
It uses Firebase for Authentication and storing your profile securly in the cloud!


Privacy Policy

All your information is transmitted securly via Firebase
It will never be used for any kind of targetted Ads
It will never be sold to anyone!
